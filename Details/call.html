The Call Service Pricing subscription delivers a transparent investment in excellence, with three plans tailored to your call support requirements. The Basic Plan at $250/month supports 3000-5000 calls per person per month, includes a custom call team, one dedicated personal agent, coverage of monthly calling bills and VPN charges, and a detailed monthly call report in xlsx format, with a 50% advance payment per person at the month’s start. The Standard Plan at $370/month builds on the Basic Plan by adding a second agent, eliminating headphone and calling bill charges, and allowing an 8-hour duty time (including rest), also with a 50% advance payment. The Premium Plan at $450/month enhances the Standard features with a bonus system implemented through KPIs to encourage higher-value results, includes advance adjustment in the call service, and features special agent monitoring, requiring a 50% advance payment. Each plan ensures top-tier service and adaptability.
